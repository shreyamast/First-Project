Honey Chilli Potato

INGREDIENTS

- Servings 4
For Potatoes
  • 4 large potatoes, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
For Honey Chilli sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons spring onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons red chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Sesame seeds and chopped spring onions for garnish
  1. In a bowl, coat the potato strips with cornflour and a pinch of salt. Ensure that each strip is well coated. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the coated potato strips until golden brown and crispy. Remove them from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.


  2. In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for a minute until aromatic. Add finely chopped spring onions and sauté for another minute. Add red chili sauce, soy sauce, tomato ketchup, honey, vinegar, and salt to taste. Mix well and let the sauce simmer for 2-3 minutes until it thickens slightly.


  3. Add the fried potato strips to the sauce. Toss them until each strip is coated evenly with the honey chilli sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Transfer the Honey Chilli Potatoes to a serving dish. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped spring onions. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Honey Chilli Potato!
