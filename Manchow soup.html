Manchow soup

INGREDIENTS

- Servings 4
FOR SOUP
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup spring onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1/4 cup carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup beans, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup capsicum, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour mixed with 1/4 cup water (slurry)
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable stock or water
For Garnish
  • Fried noodles
  • Spring onions, chopped
  1. In a pan, heat oil. Add minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for a minute until aromatic. Add finely chopped spring onions and sauté for another minute. Add finely chopped cabbage, carrots, beans, capsicum, and mushrooms. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.


  2. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and black pepper powder. Mix well. Pour in the vegetable stock or water. Bring the soup to a boil.


  3. Add the cornflour slurry to the boiling soup while stirring continuously. Cook until the soup thickens and becomes glossy. Garnish with fried noodles and chopped spring onions. Serve hot.
