Idli Sambar

INGREDIENTS

- Servings 4
FOR IDLI
  • 2 cups Idli batter
FOR Sambar
  • 1 cup Toor dal
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 drumstick, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Few curry leaves
  • Few coriander leaves for garnish
  1. For Idlis : Steam idlis using the idli batter in a steamer or idli maker until they are soft and cooked.

  2. For Sambar : Rinse toor dal and cook it with enough water until it becomes soft and mushy. Mash the dal. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent. Add tomatoes and cook until they are soft. Add sambar powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Now add the cooked dal along with chopped vegetables (carrot, drumstick, green beans). Mix well and add water if needed. Add tamarind pulp and let the sambar simmer until the vegetables are cooked and flavors are well blended. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

  4. Serve hot sambar with steamed idlis. Enjoy your delicious Idli Sambar! Adjust the spices and consistency according to your taste preferences.
