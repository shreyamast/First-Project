Veg Noodles

INGREDIENTS

- Servings 4
For Noodles
  • 200g noodles (egg or rice noodles)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup green beans, sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions, chopped for garnish (optional)
  1. Cook the Noodles : Boil the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.


  2. Prepare the Veggies : Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add sliced onions and minced garlic. Stir-fry for a minute until aromatic. Add julienned carrots, sliced bell peppers, shredded cabbage, and sliced green beans. Stir-fry the vegetables for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.


  3. Make the Sauce : In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, chili sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper. Adjust the quantities according to your taste preferences.


  4. Add the cooked and drained noodles to the wok with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss everything together using a pair of tongs or chopsticks to ensure even coating of the sauce. Stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until the noodles are heated through and well-coated with the sauce.
